How to Apply for Your Free Internship

Follow these easy steps to get started:

  1. Step 1: Click on "Apply Now" for any domain that interests you.
  2. Step 2: Fill out the quick Google Form with your details.
  3. Step 3: Join our WhatsApp Committee group for updates and support.
  4. Step 4: Complete the course and hands-on project shared in the PPT.
  5. Step 5: Pay Rs. 100 for the certificate once you finish the tasks.

That's it! You're all set to start your free internship journey.
